Privacy Policy

moprox spending tracker · last updated 27 June 2026

moprox is a private, personal application operated by a single individual (the "Operator") for the sole purpose of aggregating the Operator's own financial accounts. It has no other users.

What it accesses

With the Operator's explicit consent, the application reads account and transaction information (balances, transactions, account details) on a read-only basis, via Enable Banking, a regulated Account Information Service Provider (AISP), and only for accounts the Operator links himself. It cannot move money or make payments.

How data is handled

Account data is stored on the Operator's own self-hosted infrastructure and is used only to track and categorise the Operator's personal spending. It is never sold, shared, published, or disclosed to any third party. No other person's data is processed.

Consent & revocation

Access is granted by the Operator's explicit consent during each bank's authorisation flow and can be withdrawn at any time — by revoking consent at the bank, or by deleting the connection. Consent is time-limited and re-confirmed periodically as required by law.
